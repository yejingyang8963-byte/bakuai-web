## Tips for engaging in comments

- HN audience cares about: actual numbers (RAM, tokens/sec, battery), real privacy guarantees vs marketing, technical trade-offs.
- HN audience hates: marketing speak, "revolutionary," anything sounding LLM-generated.
- Be willing to admit weaknesses (Android missing, iPhone 13 Pro+ requirement, large download).
- Don't argue defensively. If someone disagrees, ack the point and reply with data.

## What to expect

- Best case: 100+ points, 200+ comments, on front page for 4-8 hours, 5k+ visits → 50-200 App Store conversions in 24h
- Median case: 20-50 points, no front page, 500-1000 visits, 5-20 conversions
- Worst case: ignored. Try a different time slot 1 month later.
